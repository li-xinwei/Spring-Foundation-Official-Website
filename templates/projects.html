Summer Visit

Summer Visit is a weekly volunteer teaching program that supports underprivileged children in China. Since 2012, Spring Foundation has incorporated cutting-edge perspectives and techniques in education into the program to deliver the most for our students.

Read With Me

Read with Me is a monthly online reading program that connects one volunteer to one child in rural China. Every week during the program, volunteers connect with their little reading partners to discuss their reading material and provoke insights from their students.

Flow

FLOW is committed to providing comprehensive financial and academic support to young girls in rural areas of China. Ensure that 27 girls from poor areas in Guangxi, Yunnan, and Sichuan have the resources and opportunities they need to realize their potential.

Mume Program

Mume Program provides personalized tutoring for rural students, helping them overcome academic challenges, broaden their horizons, and grow into well-rounded global citizens.
