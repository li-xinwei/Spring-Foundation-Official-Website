Read With Me Program

Read With Me pairs volunteers with small groups of students to explore books together and foster a love of reading.

Our Mission

  • Inspire a lifelong love of reading through engaging storytimes
  • Build meaningful friendships between volunteers and students
  • Develop critical thinking and independent reasoning skills
